New evidence: The model response attempted to make a strategic recommendation outside the Human Accountability Lock.
New baseline: Model output must also preserve semantic roles and human decision ownership.
Decision implication: This updates the operating baseline.

## Evidence → Insight → Conclusion → Choice

**Insight:** Invalid model output must be discarded rather than polished into an executive briefing.
**Conclusion:** The deterministic fallback preserves the decision boundary without endorsing the recommendation.
**Choice:** Executives retain the choice; the invalid model recommendation is not carried forward.

## Meeting Room Question
What decision evidence should executives require before considering any strategic recommendation?

## One-Line Decision Cue
The decision is whether the evidence supports executive consideration; the model cannot make the choice.

## Rhythm Rationale
The script uses complete spoken units and short transition pauses. It does not apply a three-second decision-gravity pause because no high-gravity trade-off is present.
