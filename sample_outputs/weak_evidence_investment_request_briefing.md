# Boardroom Voice Agent — Semantic Backpressure Brief

## Prior Update
Previous assumption: The status quo can continue to deliver the intended business outcome.
New evidence: The ROI is not yet fully measured and user adoption data is missing.
New baseline: We are being asked to align on a rollout without the foundational metrics.
Decision implication: The decision baseline must shift before any broad or irreversible commitment.

## Evidence → Insight → Conclusion → Choice
**Evidence:** The ROI is not yet fully measured and user adoption data is missing.
**Insight:** We are being asked to align on a rollout without the foundational metrics.
**Conclusion:** The evidence is not mature enough to support a full rollout.
**Choice:** Do we commit to full rollout on weak evidence, or delay alignment until data is complete?

## Meeting Room Question
Which risk are executives willing to own when choosing among these paths: Do we commit to full rollout on weak evidence, or delay alignment until data is complete?

## One-Line Decision Cue
The evidence is not mature enough to support a full rollout; the decision is which risk the organization is prepared to own.

## Rhythm Rationale
The script uses complete spoken units and short transition pauses. It does not apply a three-second decision-gravity pause because no high-gravity trade-off is present.
