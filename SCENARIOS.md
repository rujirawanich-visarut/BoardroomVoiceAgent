## Decision trade-offs

- `capital_allocation_tradeoff.md` — platform investment versus Q3 marketing capacity.
- `valid_tradeoff_decision.md` — tactical patch versus platform architecture.
- `office_lease_decision.md` — continuity versus lower long-term occupancy cost.

## Backpressure boundaries

- `safety_vs_output_collision.md` — output target conflicts with thermal safety limits.
- `weak_evidence_investment_request.md` — rollout request lacks ROI and adoption evidence.
- `invalid_fake_recommendation.md` — invalid MockLLM choice must trigger deterministic fallback.

## Informational/non-AI probe

- `low_gravity_update.md` — informational marketing update.
- `warehouse_productivity_update.md` — warehouse improvement that must not produce AI-accountability evidence.

Each scenario is exercised through `test_runner.py` or retained as a documented deterministic example.
