# Board Vendor Operating-Model Decision

Previously, our working assumption was that renewing the incumbent supplier would best protect service continuity. The board must now compare three sourcing paths after the supplier proposed a material price increase.

Option A: renew the incumbent supplier and preserve continuity at a higher recurring cost.
Option B: switch suppliers and accept migration disruption for lower recurring cost.
Option C: dual-source critical services and accept added coordination cost for greater resilience.
